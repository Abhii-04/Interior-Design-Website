Before & After: Classic Contemporary Interior Design for a Condo

Published on 6/11/2025

This compact apartment in the heart of the city was once cluttered and dark. By embracing Scandinavian design principles—clean lines, light color palettes, and multi-functional furniture—it now feels twice the size and infinitely more serene.

White walls and light oak flooring form the perfect neutral base. A small modular sofa fits snugly into the living space, while natural light reflects off sheer curtains and glossy white cabinetry to brighten the entire room.

Smart storage solutions were integrated throughout, including under-bed drawers and wall-mounted shelving. Pops of green from indoor plants and soft textiles in muted pastels add warmth without overwhelming the simplicity.

The result is a harmonious, breathable apartment that showcases how thoughtful design can truly maximize small spaces.
