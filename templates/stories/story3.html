Before & After: Classic Contemporary Interior Design for a Condo

Published on 6/11/2025

Discover the transformation of a dull, outdated condo into a modern masterpiece. With a blend of clean lines, warm neutrals, and elegant accents, this classic contemporary design project showcases the power of thoughtful interior planning.

The living room now boasts floor-to-ceiling windows framed by sheer drapes, allowing natural light to flood the space. A sleek, low-profile sofa and geometric coffee table provide balance and comfort.

The bedroom features minimalistic wall paneling with warm lighting. Textured rugs and subtle metallic decor enhance the richness without cluttering the space.
