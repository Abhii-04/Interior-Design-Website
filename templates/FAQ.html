Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our interior design services, pricing, and process.

What is Decorilla and how does it work?

Decorilla is an online interior design service that connects you with professional interior designers to create beautiful and functional spaces. You start by filling out a simple questionnaire about your style and needs, then designers create proposals for you to choose from. Once you select a designer, they work with you remotely to develop a design concept, create 3D renderings, and provide a shopping list. You can then purchase items at exclusive discounts.

What kind of spaces can Decorilla design?

We design a wide range of residential and commercial spaces, including living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, bathrooms, home offices, nurseries, and more. Whether you need a single room refresh or a full home makeover, our designers can help.

What is the experience of your interior designers?

Our interior designers are hand-picked professionals with years of experience and expertise in various design styles. They come from diverse backgrounds and are skilled in creating personalized spaces that reflect our clients' unique tastes and lifestyles. You can view their portfolios and client reviews before choosing your designer.

How long does the design process take?

The design process duration varies depending on the complexity of your project and your responsiveness. Typically, the initial concepts are delivered within 1-2 weeks, and the complete design can be finalized within 3-6 weeks. We work at your pace to ensure you're completely satisfied.

Can I provide my own furniture or decor items?

Absolutely! Our designers are happy to incorporate your existing furniture, art, or decor pieces into the new design. Just let us know during the initial questionnaire or consultation, and provide photos of the items you wish to keep.

What kind of deliverables will I receive?

Depending on your chosen package, you will receive deliverables such as concept boards, 2D floor plans, 3D realistic renderings of your space, a detailed shopping list with links to purchase products, and implementation guides. Some packages also include direct communication with your designer and project management.

How much does Decorilla's service cost?

Our pricing is transparent and based on the scope of your project. We offer various packages tailored to different needs and budgets, from single room designs to full home transformations. Please visit our Pricing page for detailed information on our packages and what's included.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, there are no hidden fees. Our pricing is upfront, and you only pay for the design package you choose. Any additional services or revisions beyond your package's scope would be discussed and agreed upon beforehand.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and PayPal for secure and convenient payments.

I'm having trouble uploading my photos. What should I do?

If you're experiencing issues with photo uploads, please ensure your images are in a supported format (JPG, JPEG, PNG) and are not excessively large. You can also try clearing your browser's cache and cookies or using a different browser. If the problem persists, please contact our support team with details about the issue and any error messages you receive.

My 3D renderings are not loading properly. What's wrong?

This could be due to a few reasons. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Large rendering files might take some time to load, especially on slower connections. Also, try updating your web browser to the latest version or using a different browser. If the issue continues, please reach out to our technical support, providing information about your device and browser.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@decorilla.com or by calling us at [Your Phone Number]. Our support hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help! Feel free to reach out to us directly.
