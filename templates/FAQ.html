Frequently Asked Questions

Explore and get to know more about Decorilla's interior design services. Find answers to all your questions about our process, pricing, designers, and more.

What is Decorilla?

Decorilla is an online interior design service that connects you with experienced interior designers who create custom room designs based on your style, budget, and needs. We offer a convenient and affordable alternative to traditional interior design services.

Our platform allows you to work with top interior designers remotely, receive multiple design concepts, and shop for furniture and decor with exclusive discounts.

How does online interior design work?

Our online interior design process is simple:

1. Start by taking our style quiz and telling us about your space, preferences, and budget.

2. Get matched with designers who specialize in your style and receive multiple design concepts.

3. Work one-on-one with your favorite designer to refine your design.

4. Receive your final design package including a 3D model, shopping list, and implementation guide.

5. Shop for your items with our exclusive discounts and transform your space!

What areas do you service?

Decorilla provides online interior design services worldwide! Since our service is completely online, we can work with clients anywhere. Our designers are based in various locations around the globe, bringing diverse perspectives and expertise to your project.

What makes Decorilla different from other online design services?

Decorilla stands out from other online interior design services in several ways:

- Multiple design concepts from different designers

- Realistic 3D renderings of your space

- Exclusive trade discounts (up to 45% off retail prices)

- Curated designer selection based on your style

- Comprehensive design packages

- One-on-one communication with your designer

- Flexible packages to suit different needs and budgets

How much does Decorilla cost?

Decorilla offers different packages to suit various needs and budgets. Our packages start at $449 per room for the Bronze package, $549 for Silver, and $849 for Gold. Each package includes multiple design concepts, 3D renderings, a shopping list, and implementation guidance.

The difference between packages is primarily in the experience level of designers you'll work with and the level of detail in your final design package.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes! One of the biggest benefits of working with Decorilla is our exclusive trade discounts. Our clients receive up to 45% off retail prices at over 350 furniture and decor retailers. These savings often cover the cost of our design services.

We also occasionally offer promotional discounts on our design packages. Sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about special offers.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your initial design concepts, you can request a full refund within 3 days of receiving them. After this period, if you're not satisfied with your designer's work, we'll match you with a new designer at no additional cost.

Do I have to purchase furniture through Decorilla?

No, you're not required to purchase furniture or decor through Decorilla. We provide you with a complete shopping list, and you're free to purchase items wherever you prefer.

However, by using our shopping links, you'll benefit from our exclusive trade discounts at many retailers, which can save you a significant amount of money.

How long does the design process take?

The typical timeline for our design process is:

- Initial design concepts: 5-7 days after completing your design brief

- Revisions: 2-4 days per revision round

- Final design package: 2-3 days after approval of the final design

Most projects are completed within 2-3 weeks, though this can vary based on the complexity of the project and the number of revision rounds needed.

What information do I need to provide?

To get started, you'll need to provide:

- Photos of your space

- Measurements of your room

- Your style preferences (our style quiz helps with this)

- Budget information

- Any existing pieces you want to keep

- Specific requirements or challenges

The more information you provide, the better your designer can understand your needs and create a design that's perfect for you.

Can I keep some of my existing furniture?

Absolutely! Many of our clients want to incorporate existing pieces into their new design. Simply let your designer know which items you want to keep, and they'll integrate them into your design.

It's helpful to provide photos and measurements of these pieces so your designer can accurately include them in your 3D renderings.

How many revisions can I request?

All of our packages include unlimited revisions during the design process. We want to ensure you're completely satisfied with your final design.

Most clients go through 2-3 rounds of revisions before approving their final design, but you can request as many changes as needed until you're happy with the result.

Who are Decorilla's designers?

Decorilla works with a curated network of professional interior designers from around the world. Our designers have diverse backgrounds, styles, and expertise, allowing us to match you with the perfect designer for your project.

All Decorilla designers are vetted for their experience, portfolio quality, and client satisfaction. Many have formal education in interior design and years of professional experience.

How are designers matched to my project?

We match designers to your project based on several factors:

- Your style preferences (determined through our style quiz)

- The type of space you're designing

- Your budget

- Designer expertise and specialization

This matching process ensures you work with designers who understand your vision and have experience with similar projects.

Can I choose my designer?

Yes! After receiving initial design concepts from multiple designers, you can choose your favorite to work with for the remainder of your project.

You can also browse our designer profiles and request specific designers when starting your project, though availability may vary.

How do I communicate with my designer?

Communication with your designer happens through our online platform. You'll have a dedicated project board where you can exchange messages, share inspiration, provide feedback, and review designs.

Depending on your package, you may also have the option for video calls with your designer for more direct communication.

What's included in my final design package?

Your final design package includes:

- Realistic 3D renderings of your space

- A detailed floor plan

- A complete shopping list with links and prices

- Implementation guidelines

- Color palette and material specifications

- Designer notes and recommendations

Higher-tier packages may include additional items like multiple layout options, lighting plans, or custom furniture recommendations.

How accurate are the 3D renderings?

Our 3D renderings are highly realistic and accurately represent how your space will look after implementation. We use professional 3D modeling software to create detailed visualizations that include your exact room dimensions, lighting conditions, and selected furniture and decor items.

These renderings help you visualize the final result before making any purchases or changes to your space.

Can I access my design after the project is complete?

Yes, you'll have permanent access to your design package through your Decorilla account. You can log in anytime to view your designs, shopping list, and all project communications.

This allows you to implement your design at your own pace and refer back to the materials whenever needed.

What if I need help implementing my design?

Your design package includes implementation guidelines to help you bring your design to life. If you need additional assistance, we offer implementation support services that can help with:

- Coordinating furniture deliveries

- Finding contractors for any necessary work

- Styling and accessorizing your space

- Virtual consultations during the implementation phase

These services can be added to your package for an additional fee.
